Common Kilogram Conversions

Metric System

The metric system uses grams and kilograms as the base units for weight. It's a decimal system that makes conversions easy.

  • • 1 metric ton = 1,000 kilograms
  • • 1 kilogram = 1,000 grams
  • • 1 gram = 1,000 milligrams
  • • 1 milligram = 1,000 micrograms

Imperial System

The imperial system uses pounds and ounces as primary weight units, still commonly used in the United States.

  • • 1 short ton = 2,000 pounds
  • • 1 pound = 16 ounces
  • • 1 ounce = 28.35 grams
  • • 1 stone = 14 pounds

Understanding Weight Conversion

Weight conversion means changing a measurement from one unit of weight to another. There are two main systems used worldwide:

Metric system – uses milligrams (mg), grams (g), kilograms (kg), and metric tons (t).

Imperial/US customary system – uses ounces (oz), pounds (lbs), stones (st), and tons.

A weight converter calculator does the math instantly and accurately. However, understanding the conversion formulas helps you verify results and understand how different weight units relate to each other.

Common Weight Conversions

Kilograms to Pounds / KG to LBS

Formula: pounds = kilograms × 2.20462

Example: 75 kg × 2.20462 = 165.35 pounds

Pounds to Kilograms / LBS to KG

Formula: kilograms = pounds ÷ 2.20462

Example: 150 pounds ÷ 2.20462 = 68.04 kg

Grams to Ounces / G to OZ

Formula: ounces = grams ÷ 28.3495

Example: 250 grams ÷ 28.3495 = 8.82 ounces

Ounces to Grams / OZ to G

Formula: grams = ounces × 28.3495

Example: 10 ounces × 28.3495 = 283.5 grams

Pounds to Ounces / LBS to OZ

Formula: ounces = pounds × 16

Ounces to Pounds / OZ to LBS

Formula: pounds = ounces ÷ 16

Kilograms to Grams / KG to G

Formula: grams = kilograms × 1000

Grams to Kilograms / G to KG

Formula: kilograms = grams ÷ 1000

Metric Tons to Pounds / Tonnes to LBS

Formula: pounds = metric tons × 2204.62

Example: 2 metric tons × 2204.62 = 4409.24 pounds

Pounds to Metric Tons / LBS to Tonnes

Formula: metric tons = pounds ÷ 2204.62

Example: 5000 pounds ÷ 2204.62 = 2.27 metric tons

Short Tons to Pounds / US Tons to LBS

Formula: pounds = short tons × 2000

Example: 1.5 short tons × 2000 = 3000 pounds

1 US short ton equals 2000 pounds exactly.

Kilograms to Ounces / KG to OZ

Stones to Pounds / ST to LBS

Formula: pounds = stones × 14

1 stone equals 14 pounds exactly.

Pounds to Stones / LBS to ST

Formula: stones = pounds ÷ 14

Example: 140 pounds ÷ 14 = 10 stones

Metric Weight Conversion

The metric system for weight is based on the gram, with larger and smaller units in multiples of 1000:

1 microgram = 0.001 milligram
1 milligram = 0.001 gram
1 gram = 0.001 kilogram
1 kilogram = 0.001 metric ton

Example: 5000 grams ÷ 1000 = 5 kg

Imperial Weight Conversion

The imperial system has several different relationships between units:

1 ounce = 1/16 pound
1 pound = 16 ounces
1 stone = 14 pounds
1 short ton = 2000 pounds
1 long ton = 2240 pounds

Frequently Asked Questions About Weight Conversion

What is the easiest way to convert weight units?

The easiest way is to use an online weight converter calculator. Enter the number, choose your units, and get the converted value instantly with high precision.

How do I convert kg to pounds without a calculator?

pounds = kilograms × 2.2 (rounded). Example: 70 kg × 2.2 = 154 pounds. For exact results, use × 2.20462.

How do I convert pounds to kg?

kilograms = pounds ÷ 2.20462. Example: 180 pounds ÷ 2.20462 = 81.65 kg.

How many ounces are in a pound?

1 pound equals exactly 16 ounces.

How do I convert grams to ounces?

ounces = grams ÷ 28.3495. Example: 100 grams ÷ 28.3495 = 3.53 ounces.

How do I convert ounces to grams?

grams = ounces × 28.3495. Example: 8 ounces × 28.3495 = 226.8 grams.

What is the formula to convert tons to pounds?

For US short tons: pounds = tons × 2000. For metric tons: pounds = metric tons × 2204.62.

How many grams are in a kilogram?

1 kilogram equals exactly 1000 grams.

How do I convert kg to grams?

grams = kilograms × 1000. Example: 2.5 kg × 1000 = 2500 grams.

How many pounds are in a stone?

1 stone equals exactly 14 pounds.

How do I convert stones to kg?

First convert stones to pounds (× 14), then convert pounds to kg (÷ 2.20462). Example: 10 stones × 14 ÷ 2.20462 = 63.5 kg.

What is a metric ton compared to a regular ton?

A metric ton (tonne) equals 1000 kg or 2204.62 pounds. A US short ton equals 2000 pounds exactly.

How do I convert kg to ounces?

ounces = kilograms × 35.274. Example: 3 kg × 35.274 = 105.82 ounces.

Can I use a weight converter for cooking measurements?

Yes, weight converters are perfect for cooking. Convert recipe measurements between grams, ounces, pounds, and kilograms accurately.

How accurate is an online weight conversion calculator?

If it uses standard conversion factors, it will be accurate to many decimal places, suitable for most practical applications.

What is the difference between weight and mass?

Mass measures the amount of matter in an object, while weight measures gravitational force. For everyday conversions, the terms are used interchangeably.

How do I convert between different types of tons?

US short ton = 2000 lbs. Metric ton (tonne) = 2204.62 lbs. Long ton = 2240 lbs. Convert through pounds as the common unit.

Can a weight conversion calculator handle very small or large weights?

Yes, from micrograms to metric tons, weight converters handle the full range of weight measurements used in science, industry, and daily life.

Why do some countries use kg and others use pounds?

Most countries use the metric system (kg), while the United States primarily uses the imperial system (pounds). The UK uses a mix of both systems.

How do I convert body weight between kg and pounds?

For body weight: pounds = kg × 2.2 (simple), or kg × 2.20462 (precise). Example: 70 kg = 154 lbs.
